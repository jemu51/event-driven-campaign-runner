# Example Prompts

## Example 1
Buyer buyer-002 wants to launch a new campaign targeting the Chicago and Atlanta markets.
The campaign requires 5 providers per market.
Providers must have the required equipment: spectrum analyzer.
Providers must submit the required document: insurance certificate (no minimum coverage specified).
Travel is required for this campaign.

## Example 2

We’re starting a new campaign for buyer-002 and looking for providers in Chicago and Atlanta.
We need five providers in each market who are ready to travel as part of the job.

To qualify, providers should have a spectrum analyzer and be able to submit an insurance certificate with a minimum coverage of $2,000.
